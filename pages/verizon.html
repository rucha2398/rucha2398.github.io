Verizon

Helping customers reach all of their security goals and needs in one centralized place.










The Problem


Within the Verizon ecosystem, there are dozens of apps for their customers. The client was looking to create a centralized dashboard for users to setup, view and manage all of their security settings. Over the months, we helped design a setup experience, dashboard experience, as well as an experience to view overall security stats and ratings based on your profile.




The Process


My focus for this project was the smart security setup for the account admin. I spent a lot of time researching in-app security setup experiences. I also took a step back to look at overarching goals and opportunity areas for this project. These goals were crucial as a benchmark for our designs.
